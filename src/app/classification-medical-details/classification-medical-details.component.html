Disability and Medical Details

Disability Classification

If you are a swimmer with a disabiity classification, please select your classification. If you do not already have a classification you will need apply for one before you can enter an event as a disabled swimmer.

If you do not know what this means, please leave the default option of "No Classification" selected.

Medical

If you have a medical condition that may affect your swimming please declare it here. Medical conditions we need to know about are conditions that may affect your ability to swim a stroke according to the rules or which may be important to your safety.

Please note this information will be provided to the Meet Director, the referees and other meet officials for the purpose of correctly judging your stroke and for safety purposes.

Please bring your medical certificate to present to the Meet Referee.
A medical certificate is usually required for a technique dispensation. Please contact the Meet Director or Meet Referee to discuss your condition.
