Entry Instructions

Please check the rules for the meet shown below. Select the checkbox beside each event you wish to enter.

Most events will require you to provide a seed time, which is the time you expect to achieve in the event. This seed time will be used to put you in a heat with swimmers who expect to swim a similar time. You may supply your own time, or if you have swum in previous MSQ meets you can click one of the preset options:

  • PB: This automatically loads your age group Personal Best time for this event
  • LT: This automatically loads the last time you swam in the event
  • Other options are available by clicking the arrow

If you supply no time you'll be placed in the slowest heat of the event. Note: you can supply a seed time even if it's the first time you've competed in this event. Just choose a time you've recorded in training.

Meet Rules:

You may enter 5 events.
